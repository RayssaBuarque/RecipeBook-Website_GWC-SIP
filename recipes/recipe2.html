Potato, pea & egg curry rotis

  • Home
  • Recipes

Ingredients:

  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 2 tbsp mild curry paste
  • 400g can chopped tomatoes
  • 2 potatoes, cut into small chunks
  • 200g peas
  • 3 eggs, hard-boiled
  • pack rotis, warmed through
  • 150g tub natural yogurt, to serve

Preparation

Heat the oil in a saucepan and briefly fry the curry paste.
Tip in the tomatoes and half a can of water and bring to a simmer.
Add the potatoes and cook for 20 mins, or until the potato is tender.
Stir in the peas and cook for 3 mins.
Halve the eggs and place them on top of the curry, then warm everything through.
Serve with the rotis and yogurt on the side.
